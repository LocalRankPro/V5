NAP Consistency Checker

Verify your Name, Address, Phone is consistent everywhere

What is NAP? NAP = Name, Address, Phone. Google uses NAP to verify your business. Even small differences like "St" vs "Street" or "+92" vs "0" can confuse Google and hurt your rankings. Your NAP must be exactly identical everywhere.
Common NAP Mistakes to Avoid:
❌ "Street" in one place, "St" in another
❌ "+92-300" vs "0300" phone format
❌ "Lahore" vs "Lahore, Punjab"
❌ Showing phone as "03001234567" vs "030-012-34567"

Save a Business Profile to run the NAP Checker.
